Coco Banana Bars

* 1.25 cup oats
* 1 cup hot milk
* 2 bananas
* 2 eggs
* salt (pinch)
* 4 tbsp cocoa
* 1tsp baking powder
* .75 cup walnuts
* 100g milk chocolate


Recipe

1. Mix oats and milk, steep for 10min
2. Mash bananas
3. Mix eggs into bananas
4. Add cocoa, salt, and baking powder to mixture
5. Combine All
6. Grease pan with butter
7. Add batter to pan
8. Top with walnuts
9. Bake 35-40min @ 360Â°F
10. melt chocolate and drizzle on top
11. chill
